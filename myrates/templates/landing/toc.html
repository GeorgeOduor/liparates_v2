About Us

Liparates.com is an innovative online platform dedicated to assisting landowners across all counties in Kenya. We streamline the process of obtaining invoices from respective county offices for the payment of land rates and facilitate comprehensive land search services. Our goal is to make land management easier, more transparent, and accessible for everyone.

Our Values

  • Integrity: Upholding the highest standards of honesty and transparency in all our services.
  • Efficiency: Delivering prompt and accurate services to save our clients time and effort.
  • Customer-Centricity: Prioritizing the needs and satisfaction of our customers in every interaction.
  • Innovation: Continuously improving our technology and processes to offer the best solutions.
  • Reliability: Ensuring consistent and dependable services that our clients can trust.

Our Vision

To be the leading platform in Kenya for land management services, transforming the way landowners interact with county offices and access vital information.

Our Mission

To empower landowners in Kenya by providing easy access to essential land-related services, ensuring convenience, accuracy, and efficiency in managing land rates and searches.

Tagline

"Efficient and Effortless."

Our Aim

Our aim is to revolutionize land management in Kenya by offering a user-friendly platform that simplifies the process of obtaining land rates invoices and conducting land searches, thereby fostering transparency, efficiency, and ease of access for all landowners.
